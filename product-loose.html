This potent blend is designed to holistically support your daily routine. The robust loose leaf cut allows maximum extraction of gingerols (aiding digestion) and polyphenols from the black tea for immense cellular support and sustained energy.

Brewing Instructions

1. Scoop 1 heaping teaspoon per 8oz of water into a steeper.
2. Pour 205°F water over the leaves.
3. Let it steep for 4 minutes to allow the spices to bloom.
4. Strain and mindfully enjoy.
